HyperMedia

Hypermedia extends hypertext by linking more than plain text. Images, sound, and video can all become part of a connected information system.

Graphics

Graphics add diagrams, illustrations, and interface elements to linked documents.

Video

Video allows moving images and demonstrations to become part of a hypermedia experience.

Sound

Sound supports audio explanations, narration, and media-rich presentation.
