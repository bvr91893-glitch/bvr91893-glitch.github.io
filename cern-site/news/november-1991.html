November W3 News

This summary collects the kinds of updates that circulated as the WorldWideWeb project matured: browser improvements, wider testing, and growing interest from other institutions.

  • Prototype browsers continued to improve on NeXTStep and line-mode systems.
  • More colleagues were able to try linked documents and keyword search services.
  • Documentation and demonstrations helped introduce the project to a wider audience.
