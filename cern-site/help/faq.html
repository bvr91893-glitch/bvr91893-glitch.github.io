Frequently Asked Questions

What is WorldWideWeb?

It is a hypertext information system designed to connect documents across different computers and networks.

Who is it for?

It began with researchers and support groups, but the design allows broader public information sharing.

How do I use it?

You use a browser to follow links, search indexes, and move between related documents.

Where can I learn more?

See the executive summary and the browser guide.
