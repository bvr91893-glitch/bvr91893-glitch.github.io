One of the most interesting parts of this project was creating emotional landscapes as someone who is blind. While I cannot visually see facial expressions or scenery the same way sighted people can, I can hear emotion in voices, sound, weather, and atmosphere. This assignment challenged me to translate emotional feelings into environmental storytelling using AI prompts. As I continued working, my prompts became more detailed and focused on mood, lighting, weather, animals, and composition. I am most proud of the loneliness image because the moonlit environment and isolated puppy created a strong emotional feeling through the landscape itself.
