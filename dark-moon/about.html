About Dark Moon Consulting

Dark Moon Consulting was formed with one purpose in mind: consulting. Not just in the traditional sense—but as a voice for inclusion, clarity, and access.

We’ve worked in accessibility since the early days of the internet, offering services in digital equity, legal strategy, and even war consulting. Our team believes accessibility isn’t just a checkbox. It’s a way of designing for humanity.

Our Promise

We serve people of all races, ages, and financial backgrounds—from the most privileged to the most vulnerable. We work, so you don’t have to fear it.

Meet the Founder

Bryan Roache, Founder of Dark Moon Consulting, is an accessibility advocate, IT student, and public policy thinker. As a blind person living with cerebral palsy and kyphosis, Bryan brings real-world understanding to every consultation. Currently studying Information Technology at NOVA, he launched Dark Moon after being turned away from services—vowing to be the advocate others didn’t have.
