About Me

I am currently pursuing an Associate of Applied Science in Information Technology with a focus on Help Desk support at Northern Virginia Community College. My goal is to build strong technical troubleshooting skills and eventually expand into advanced networking and systems support.

I also do consulting work centered on accessibility and practical technology decisions. I have helped companies think through more accessible platforms, including work connected to Braille debit cards, and I have helped individuals find accounts and services that work best for their needs.

As a consultant, I understand that the world is not always built with blind people in mind. My work starts from that reality and focuses on tools, services, and platforms that people can actually use.
